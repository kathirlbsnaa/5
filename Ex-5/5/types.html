TYPES OF COFFEE BEAN

  • Coffee is a brewed drink made from coffee beans, which are actually the roasted seeds of the coffee plant. The species of bean used, where it was sourced from, and how it was roasted will all affect the taste and aroma of the final cup you'll sip.

  • The two main categories of coffee beans you'll come across are Arabica and Robusta.
